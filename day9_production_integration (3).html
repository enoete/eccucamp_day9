Troubleshooting Common Issues

üîß Quick Fixes for Common Problems

  • Audio out of sync: Manually align audio waveforms with video
  • Video quality loss: Check export settings, use higher bitrate
  • Choppy playback: Lower preview quality during editing
  • Large file sizes: Compress video or reduce resolution
  • Color mismatch: Apply same color filter to all clips
  • Harsh transitions: Add crossfade or motion blur
